ZKProof

A Zero Knowledge Proof system is a cryptographic protocol by which a party (the prover) can prove, without revealing any underlying information, to another party (the verifier) that a certain statement is true. This breakthrough technology enables industries and individuals to go beyond the trade-off between data privacy and integrity.

ZKProof.org is an open consortium of industry and academia to standardize the use of zero knowledge proofs.

Please join our mailing list and contribute to the ZKProof community and our standardization efforts.
