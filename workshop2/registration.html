Please submit this form and tell us why you would like to participate in the next ZKProof event.

If you would like to sponsor this event, please see here and email us at contact@zkproof.org

Next workshop in Berkeley, CA, on April 10th to 12th 2019

The 2nd ZKProof Workshop will take place over three days in Berkeley CA. If you would like to attend please fill up the form below. All relevant applicants will receive a follow up invitation to register for the workshop. Registration may be partially covered by our generous sponsors.
During the first two days, there will be a Standards Workshop, where participants will discuss and build an initial set of community standards. The mornings will feature keynote and invited speakers from both academic and industry backgrounds and the evenings breakout sessions for community discussions.
The third day will have an Industry & Research Showcase, dedicated to existing work around zero knowledge proofs, divided into a few tracks for related tutorials, demos and presentations submitted and presented by the community.
We will be publishing a call for community standards proposals and a call for satellite events.

Quick Info

ZKProof is an open initiative of industry and academia to standardize the use of zero knowledge proofs. We are planning several workshops to standardize the security, implementation, applications and all other related aspects of this technology.
